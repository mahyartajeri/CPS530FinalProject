Animals of the African Savannah

African Elephant
The African Elephant is the largest land mammal in Africa.
Lion
The Lion is the apex predator of the continent.
Hippopotamus
The Hippo is one of world's most dangerous animals.
Cheetah
The Cheetah is the world's fastest land mammal.
Giraffe
The Giraffe has the longest neck of any land mammal.
Zebra
The Zebra is known for its unique striped pattern.
Rhino
The Rhino is critically endangered due to poaching.
African Wild Dog
The African Wild Dog hunts in packs.
